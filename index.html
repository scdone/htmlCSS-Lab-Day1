Stet's Favorite Games

Video and Board Games Ranked 1 - 5.

Video Games

#1 - Age of Empires II

Number one on the list is a classic real-time strategy game from 1999.

#2 - Old School Runescape

Number two is an online MMORPG from 2007. The game was dead for a few years until it was brought back again in 2013.

#3 - World of Warcraft

Number three welcomes players into the world of Azeroth with the second MNMORPG on this list from 2004.

#4 - Horizon Zero Dawn

Number four is our first venture on the list beyond computer games. Horizon Zero Dawn is a Playstation exclusive open world RPG launched in 2017.

#5 - Borderlands 3

Number 5 on the list is a cross-platform role-playing first-person shooter game with a quirky dystopian vibe. This title is the third in the series and was released in 2019.

Card Games and Board Games

#1 - Magic the Gathering

Overall, the greatest game ever created. Magic the Gathering is a tabletop collectible card game created by Richard Garfield in 1993.

#2 - Bohnanza

Number two is a euro card game based on trading and politics released in 1997.

#3 - Splendor

Number 3 is a euro card development game with simple mechanics and a simple, relaxing playstyle.

#4 - Forbidden Island

Number 4 is a co-op style game where every player works together to get off the island alive.

#5 - Monopoly Deal

Number 5 on the list is a reincarnation of the original Monopoly. It's a faster paced, fun game for the family.
